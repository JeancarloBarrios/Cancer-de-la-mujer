Stanley Cup Prediction

About Stanley cup Prediction

Our model uses logistic regression to predict the winner of the 2014-2015 Stanley Cup based on their regular season performance. Using data from the last 8 seasons, we analyzed the following variables:
Points %, G/G, GA/G, Goal Ratio, PP%, PK%, S/G, SA/G, Shot Ratio, FO%, Corsi, PIM, Shooting%, Save%, PDO, % Cap spent on Forwards, Defense or Goalie and Average age.
After careful analysis, we used a stepwise selection of variables to create a model using (in order of importance): PP%, Corsi, Shooting%, SA/G, GA/G and SV%.
